Hobbiton is an entrance into Middle Earth, a leap into fantasy. Here you can take a couple of different guided tours through the set of The Shire from the Lord Of The Rings and the Hobbit. You can Tour through The Shire and Green Dragon Inn and even have a banquet there.

Doubtful Sound is the longest and deepest fjord in the south island of New Zealand, complete with multiple waterfalls and even some deeper sea creatures living in the more shallow waters due to the salt content towards the bottom.

Glow worms are common in New Zealand, and they don't just live in caves. They live in many damp places. However, one of the best ways to see them is a guided boat tour through the Waitomo Caves.
